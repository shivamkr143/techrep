ABOUT US



Techniche is the annual Techno-Management festival of IIT Guwahati, India. The lecture series serves as a platform to inspire an dmotivate tousands of young minds across the world by connecting them with pioneers in various fields

chor log

Thomas Barclay

Research Scientist, NASA; Deputy Director, TESS Science Support Center: Dr. Tom Barclay is the principal Investigator and Director of the Kepler/K2 Guest Observer Office and is an expert in data analytics from space and ground based telescopes that had led to discovery of the smallest known planet and the super earth sized planet orbiting in the habitable zone of a sun like star

Nadrian Seeman

Inventor of the field of DNA Nanotechnology, Feynmann Prize winner: An american chemist and crystallographer, Nadrian Seeman, is credited for the invention of DNA nanotechnology, revolutionizing genetics. An Awardee of the Kavli Prize and the Feynman Prize, his research paved the way for further applications in the fields of DNA Computing, DNA Nanorobotics and Nanoelectronics

Brooks Moore

Host of How It’s Made, Emmy Winner: A winner of multiple Emmy, Telly and Sundace awards, Brooks Turner Moore is the person whose voice we all grew up hearing on all our favourite shows on Discovery Channel and its related networks. His most prominent work as a narrator in for the well known show "How it's made".

Mike Morasky

Visual Effects Supervisor, The Lord of the Rings and the Matrix; Music Composer, CS:GO: An animator, AV effects artist, director and programmer, Mike Morasky is best known for his work at Valve Corporations composing the scores of Portal, Portal 2 and the Left 4 Dead Series. He visual effects work on the infamous Lord of The Rings and The Matrix trilogies are also world famous.

Shri Suresh Prabhu

Ex-Railway Minister of India: Suresh Prabhakar Prabhu is an Indian politician and the former Railway Minister of India. A Chartered Accountant by profession, Mr Prabhu was an elected member of the World Bank parliamentary network, chaired the South Asia Water regional group of the World Bank and had won great praise for his performance as the Union Minister for Power.

Shri Subramanian Swamy

Member of Rajya Sabha, Harvard Professor and Economist: An economist, mathematician and a politician, Mr. Subramanian Swamy is a Member of Parliment in Rajya Sabha and a member of the BJP. A Harvard alumnus, Mr. Swamy was a part of the group of eminent persons called to Geneva, Switzerland to prepare a report of the United Nations on Economic Co-operation between Developing countries.
