ABOUT US



Techniche is the annual Techno-Management festival of IIT Guwahati, India. Started in 1999, through its past nineteen editions, it has been witnessing a new zenith of techno-management events ranging from raging robotics to corporate modules, from inspiring lecture series to awe striking workshops to jubilant nights. Techniche has been an extraordinary platform to showcase the latest developments in the fields of science and technology exhibiting the scientific advances from all over the globe.
